    The Cat House is just a typical loving home, currently caring for almost 50 cats
    and 4 dogs. It is not a re-homing center or a big facility and we do not have regular
    donors. We do not have volunteers or staff, we just work all together to provide the
    daily needs or our rescues.

    We are dedicated in finding a good homes for our rescues and other cats in need.

    We encouraged pet owners to support the neutering and spaying their pets.

    We also participate in providing information to improve people's understanding of
    cats and their care.

    We also help in promoting TNVR (trap-neuter-vaccinate-return) for strays and ferals.
