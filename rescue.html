    Rescued Date: April 25, 2012
    Given Name: Boots
    Age: 8 years old
    Sex: Female

    Year 2012 my Uncle (Mama's brother) found him on the street during the heavy rain.
    Boots was not noticed immediately because the flood was high then and it was dark
    because it was early in the morning. Boots is said to be looking for a possible climb.
    Case he could not get on the parked pedicab. He was picked up and taken to our house.

    Boots was Spayed and now an indoor cat living with us.

    Rescued Date: August 26, 2018
    Given Name:Mara
    Age:2-3 years old
    Sex: Female

    A cat needs to be rescued at Manila Memorial Park today. The nose seems to be
    rotting.Difficulty breathing and severely emaciated. The cat has a nasal tumor.
    He was thin and hard to breathe. His diet was also affected by the bait. He seems
    to have been seen in the cemetery for a long time. He only has food when people visit
    there.

    Mara was put to sleep because her nasal tumor was progressive. She also did not eat
    and had difficulty breathing. You will always be in our hearts Mara.

    Rescued Date: November 15, 2020
    Given Name:Kaypee
    Age:4 weeks old
    Sex: Female

    This kitten was rescued last November 15 while we were walking with the Kapon Pilipinas
    team near my school, kittens often really cover the area because it is dark and full of
    garbage. He also had a fever last November 20 and ate well until yesterday night we
    noticed that he was always bent over and the gums were already swollen.

    November 24, She passed away at exactly 5:49 pm. Kaypee is just one of so many animals
    that are thrown away every day. Sadly, the others are still small enough to live on the
    streets.
